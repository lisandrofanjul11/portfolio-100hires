## Post 1: Using AI for Ideation, Not Writing

**Core idea:** Use AI to find the story behind your next post — without writing a single word for you.

### The 6-Step Process

1. **Give it a topic** — Something you want to write about but can't find an angle for.

2. **It interviews you** — It starts by asking for a specific moment: a client, a conversation, something you saw or felt around that topic.

3. **If you're stuck (most people are), it walks you back through time** — Last week, last month, 3 months ago, a year, or all the way back to when you first started if need be. Each prompt gives your brain one specific place to search for an idea instead of literally anything that ever existed.

4. **If there's still no single moment, it pulls in your credibility** — The earned perspective you've built across years in your field: things only you and a few others could claim.

5. **Once it finds something real, it starts digging** — What happened right before? What did they actually say? How did you feel? What changed for you?

6. **It hands it all back as a set of notes** — The moment, detail, realisation, and the lesson.

## Post 2: The 3-Rule Framework to Drive Traffic with Links

I just proved it. You do not need to go viral to drive traffic. AND LinkedIn loves links in posts. Here's the proof from my last 21 posts:
- 455,393 impressions
- 3,213 link clicks
- 1 click for every 142 impressions

### The 3 Strict Rules:
1. Every post has 1+ link
2. 100 words maximum
3. Text, or text + one image (no fancy designs - no carousels, no infographics)

**Key Business Lesson:** Clicks grow your newsletter, lead magnet, or business. Impressions do, unfortunately, just feel good. Focus on what converts.
